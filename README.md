# Farming Village Simulator - Hand Control

A fun, interactive 3D farming game where you control a character using hand gestures detected via your webcam.

## How to Play

- **Move your hand** in front of the camera to guide your character through the village
- **Walk over yellow crops** to harvest them and earn money
- **Earn money** by collecting crops and see your score increase
